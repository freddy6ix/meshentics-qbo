Meshentics Bookkeeping

An internal application by Meshentics Technologies Inc. (Ontario, Canada)

Meshentics Bookkeeping is a private internal tool used by Meshentics Technologies Inc. to maintain its own books in QuickBooks Online. It records and classifies the company’s accounting entries via Intuit’s authorized APIs.

To connect or reconnect the application to QuickBooks Online, the authorized user runs the tool’s sign-in flow and authorizes access to their own company file.

Disconnect Meshentics Bookkeeping

To disconnect Meshentics Bookkeeping from your QuickBooks Online company, you can either:

  • In QuickBooks Online: go to Settings → Apps, find this application, and choose Disconnect; or
  • In Intuit account security settings, revoke the application’s access under connected apps.

On disconnection, the application’s stored access and refresh tokens are no longer valid and are removed locally. No further data is accessed.

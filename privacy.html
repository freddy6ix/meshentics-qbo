Privacy Policy

Application: Meshentics Bookkeeping · Publisher: Meshentics Technologies Inc. (Ontario, Canada) · Effective: June 6, 2026

Meshentics Bookkeeping is a private internal application used by Meshentics Technologies Inc. (“we,” “us”) to maintain its own books in QuickBooks Online. This policy explains what data the Application handles and how.

1. Data we access

With the user’s explicit authorization via Intuit OAuth 2.0, the Application accesses Accounting data in the connected QuickBooks Online company — chart of accounts and journal entries — solely to create and reconcile bookkeeping records. It does not access data from any company the user is not authorized to administer.

2. Source documents

The user may process their own financial statements and card exports locally to classify business expenses. These source files are handled on the user’s own machine and are not transmitted to any third party by the Application.

3. How data is used

Accessed data is used only to record and classify accounting entries for the company’s own books. We do not sell, rent, or share any accessed data with third parties, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

4. Storage and security

OAuth tokens and credentials are stored locally and are never committed to source control or shared. Access is limited to the authorized user. Connections to Intuit use encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) transport.

5. Retention

Accounting records are retained in the user’s QuickBooks Online company as required for tax and corporate record-keeping (in Canada, generally six years). Local tokens may be revoked at any time by disconnecting the Application in Intuit.

6. Contact

For privacy questions regarding this Application, contact Meshentics Technologies Inc., Ontario, Canada.
